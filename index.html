About 1stChoice Connected Limited

1stChoice Connected Limited is a Birmingham-based company focused on delivering practical services that support both businesses and individuals. We bring together security services, cleaning projects and employment support under one brand, creating real community impact while supporting clients operationally.

Our approach is professional, people-focused and growth-led. We connect people to opportunities and dependable services—whether that’s work, training, safe environments or site protection.

Our Core Service Areas

We operate across three main service areas, providing joined-up support for businesses, venues, landlords and individuals.

Security Services

SIA-licensed manned guarding, door supervision, event security, concierge and site support, mobile patrols, retail and construction site security.

Cleaning Services

Domestic and commercial cleaning, deep cleans, Airbnb turnover cleaning, HMO and landlord support, communal area cleaning and scheduled visits.

Employment Support & Apprenticeships

CV support, interview preparation, job applications, job fair events, career coaching and apprenticeship pathways in business, digital, customer service and more.
