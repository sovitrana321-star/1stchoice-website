About 1stChoice Connected Limited

1stChoice Connected Limited is a Birmingham-based company focused on delivering practical services that support both businesses and individuals. We bring together security services, cleaning projects, employment support, apprenticeship pathways and housing-bridge services under one brand, creating real community impact while supporting clients operationally.

Our approach is professional, people-focused and growth-led. We connect people to opportunities and dependable services—whether that’s work, training, safe accommodation or site protection.
