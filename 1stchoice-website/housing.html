We do not sell housing. We act as a bridge, connecting individuals to supported accommodation and property support services through trusted partners.

How Our Housing Support Works

We work alongside housing providers, landlords and support organisations to help individuals access safe, stable accommodation. Our role is to connect people to the right services and support, not to act as landlords or property sellers.

Supported Accommodation Links

We signpost individuals to supported housing options that match their needs.

Property Support Services

We work with partners who provide property management, maintenance and support.

Holistic Support

Housing support is often combined with employment support, training and other services.
